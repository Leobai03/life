# Review Template

Use these templates to keep the SOP alive after it is created.

## Daily Check-In

```text
Date:
Sleep / wake time:
Meals:
Body movement or outdoor time:
Main work action:
Creation or learning trace:
Most unstable moment:
What actually blocked me:
Score out of 10:
One adjustment for tomorrow:
```

## 7-Day Review

```text
1. Which rule helped me the most?
2. Which rule was unrealistic?
3. Which day had the best state, and what caused it?
4. Which day was most unstable, and what triggered it?
5. Did I protect my body baseline?
6. Did I complete enough work to keep self-trust alive?
7. What should be removed, kept, or tightened for next week?
```

## 14-Day Review

```text
1. Has my sleep moved in the right direction?
2. Has my food and movement baseline improved?
3. What work action gives the highest return?
4. What content or learning trace is easiest to sustain?
5. What emotional pattern repeated most often?
6. What boundary is now clearly necessary?
7. What should Life SOP v2 focus on?
```

## 30-Day Review

```text
1. Which part of the SOP became natural?
2. Which part still requires too much force?
3. What did I learn about my real priorities?
4. What should become a permanent rule?
5. What should be retired because this stage has changed?
6. What is the next-stage theme?
```

## Scoring Rule

Use scoring to observe, not punish.

- 8-10: Strong day. Keep the structure.
- 6-7: Passed. Adjust lightly.
- 4-5: Identify the main blocker and reduce tomorrow's scope.
- 0-3: Reset body baseline first. Do not compensate by overloading tomorrow.
